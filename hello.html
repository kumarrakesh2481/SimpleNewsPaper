Dhurandhar 2: The Revenge director Aditya Dhar has clarified that Ranveer Singh’s viral smoking photo is ‘fake’ and ‘AI-generated’. Hours after the Sikh group expressed disappointment with the alleged scene and filed a police complaint, Dhar took to his Instagram handle and issued an official statement. He urged everyone not to believe in AI-created fake images of Ranveer Singh’s character Jaskirat shown smoking. He also stated that he holds the utmost respect for the Sikh community and requested everyone to rely only on official content.7
